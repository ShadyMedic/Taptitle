You can export words from your personal dictionary into an Anki deck here.

Choose the style of the cards in your Anki deck:

▶

By default, your deck will contain only cards that will help you understand written foreign lagnuage. That means that the word in your chosen foreign language will always be the question and the translation into your language the answer.

If you also want to learn the foreign words, check the box below. This will add reversed cards to your deck, meaning that it'll also contain cards on which words in your language are the questions and the foreign words the answers.

Warning

After importing your vocabulary, you won't be able to update the style of the cards by importing another file generated by this tool.

You'll still be able to change the style of cards manually (tech-savvy users only). Another option would be to delete the old cards and importing the new ones. You'll, however, lose your planning data by doing this.
